# Blood Disorders and Accessibility

## Blood disorders

`site_file$blood_disorders`

Site-level frequencies of inherited blood disorders that modify malaria
risk. These are **2010 estimates**.

### Variables

`sicklecell` Sickle haemoglobin (HbS) allele frequency  
`gdp6` Glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency allele
frequency  
`hpc` Haemoglobin C (HbC) allele frequency  
`duffy_negativity` Duffy negativity phenotype frequency

### Description

Allele and phenotype frequencies are taken from the Malaria Atlas
Project blood disorder surfaces (2010 estimates). Duffy negativity is
particularly relevant for *Plasmodium vivax* transmission, while HbS,
HbC and G6PD deficiency influence *Plasmodium falciparum* disease risk.

## Accessibility

`site_file$accessibility`

Site-level estimates of travel time to healthcare and to the nearest
city, summarised from the Malaria Atlas Project accessibility surfaces.

### Variables

`motor_travel_time_healthcare` Motorised travel time to healthcare,
minutes (2019 estimate)  
`walking_travel_time_healthcare` Walking-only travel time to healthcare,
minutes (2019 estimate)  
`city_travel_time` Travel time to the nearest city, minutes (2015
estimate)

### Description

These accessibility metrics are useful for interpreting access to
treatment and for costing. Values are population-at-risk weighted
summaries over each site.
